AFWall+ - iptables GUI front-end firewall

AFWall+ - Android Firewall Plusis a front-end application for the powerful iptablesLinux firewall. It allows you to restrict which applications are permitted to access your data networks.
This is the perfect solution if you don\'t have an unlimited data plan, or just want extend battery life.
